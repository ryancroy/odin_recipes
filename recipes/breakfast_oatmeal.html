Breakfast Oatmeal

Description

Quick and easy breakfast oatmeal topped with your favorite fruits.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup fruit
  • 1 tsp honey

Steps

  1. Combine oats and milk in a microwavable bowl
  2. Microwave for 2 minutes
  3. While oatmeal is cooking, prepare fruit
  4. Check oatmeal; if milk has not been absorbed, add 30 seconds to the cook time
  5. Remove oatmeal from microwave; CAUTION: it will be very hot
  6. Let cool for 2 minutes
  7. Top with fruit and honey

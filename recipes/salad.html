Salad

Description

A delicious kale salad topped with numerous vegetables and beans for protein.

Ingredients

  • 2 stalks kale
  • 1 cup grape tomatoes
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1/4 cup garbanzo beans
  • 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tbsp sunflower seeds

Steps

Remember to rinse all ingredients prior to making salad

  1. Strip leafy kale from stalks
  2. Slice grape tomatoes in half
  3. Cube avocado into small portions
  4. Add garbanzo beans
  5. Top with olive oil and sunflower seeds

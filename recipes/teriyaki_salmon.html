Teriyaki Salmon

Description

A mouth-watering salmon filet marinated in house teriyaki sauce, served over rice and garnished with sesame seeds.

Ingredients

  • 1 salmon filet
  • 2 tbsp teriyaki sauce
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Place salmon and marinade in a shallow dish; allow fish to marinate for at least 30mins
  2. Preheat oven or grill to 400°F
  3. Bring water and rice to a boil; allow rice to cook for at least 10 minutes
  4. Put salmon on oven or grill; allow to cook for at least 12 minutes
  5. Remove rice and salmon from heat
  6. Serve salmon over rice and garnish with sesame seeds
